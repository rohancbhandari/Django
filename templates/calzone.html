Whole Wheat Pesto Calzone

June 25 , 2020

Ingredients
  • 1 cups warm water 105 to 115 degrees
  • 1-1/2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tbs honey
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup Pesto sauce
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup grated cheese(I used Italian blend)
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
Ingredients for Pesto Sauce
  • 3/4 cup pine nuts/ walnuts
  • 4 garlic cloves/li>
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan-Reggiano or Romano cheese freshly grated
  • 3 cups basil leaves packed
  • 1 cup spinach packed
  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice  
Directions
  1. Mix together the warm water, yeast, and honey. Let it sit for 5 minutes, then add the oil.
  2. In the bowl of a stand mixer, mix together the flour and the salt. Add the yeast mixture.
  3. With a dough hook, beat the mixture until a ball of dough forms. The dough should be well hydrated but not sticky. (It should be almost sticky.) If it’s sticky, add a little more flour. If it seems too dry, add a little more water.
  4. Divide the dough. If you want large calzones, make 4 evenly sized balls. If you want smaller, individual size calzones, make 8 balls. Cover the dough and let it rise for 20-30 minutes.
  5. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees and grease cookie sheet
  6. Roll out each piece of dough into a circle 1/8 inch thick. Top the dough with pesto sauce, tomatoes , olives and cheese. Or Anything you would put on a pizza is fair game.
  7. Fold the dough in half and shape as per your choice .Pierce the dough with a fork once or twice.
  8. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes, until the dough begins to turn brown. Let the calzones sit for a few minutes before cutting. Enjoy warm or at room temperature
