Ultimate Power Plate

May 10, 2020

Tahini sauce - Ingredients
  • 1 garlic clove, peeled
  • 1/2 cup well-stirred tahini(a Middle Eastern paste or sauce made from ground sesame seeds)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons water
Falafal - Ingredients
  • 6 garlic cloves, peeled
  • 1/2 cup lightly packed parsley
  • 1/2 cup lightly packed cilantro
  • 1 scallion, coarsely chopped
  • 2-3 cups chickpeas, drained, rinsed and patted dry with paper towels
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon tahini
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons oat or almond flour
  • 4-6 tablespoons neutral vegetable oil, such as grapeseed.
Directions
  1. Heat 3 tablespoons of the vegetable oil in a large non-stick or cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Arrange the falafel in the pan, leaving plenty of space in between each.
  3. Cook until golden brown on the bottom, about 2 minutes.
  4. Flip and cook until golden on the other side, 1 to 2 minutes longer.
Power Plate - Servings
  • Hummus
  • Quinoa with some herbs
  • Chopped beetroot, cucumbers and sliced avocado
  • Pan fried Tofu (you can use Paneer
  • Roasted chickpeas
