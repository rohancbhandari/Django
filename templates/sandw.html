Veggie & Olive Hummus Sandwich

June 14, 2020

Ingredients
  • Whole-grain bread
  • Black Olive Hummus
  • Avocado mashed /li>
  • Spinach
  • Tomato, Cucumber, Carrots sliced
  • Alfalfa Sprouts
Directions
  1. In a food processor add tahini (sesame seed paste), cold water, olive oil, cumin, salt, garlic and lemon.
  2. Add in the boiled and drained chickpeas
  3. Blend for 3-4 minutes, or until smooth.  Yep, put that food processor Just keep it going and going until the hummus is nice and smooth. Taste and season.  
  4. Give the hummus a taste, and add in olives
  5. If the hummus seems too thick, feel free to add in an extra tablespoon or two of water too.
  6. Now simply assemble the sandwich and enjoy!!!!
