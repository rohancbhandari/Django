Hello I'm Vrushali but my friends call me Vrush and my blog is about making everyday food with simple ingredients. I try to make home cooking easy, approachable and enjoyable. Being creative in the kitchen and eating healthy food is my passion.

I am the food stylist, recipe creator, photographer and author of Homecooking_Comfortfood. Apart from cooking I am a total hodophile,Fitness Freak inspired by bodypower.in(instagram @bodypower.in) and a fashion enthusiasts.

What started as a casual hobby for nights and weekends while I was working as a Software Engineer. Currently , I live in Phoenix, Arizona with my husband Rohan.

Happy cooking!

Where did you learn to cook ?

It’s all self taught through lots of mistakes, trials and errors. I’m pretty lucky to have culinary skills from my mom as she has always been passionate about cooking and feeding. I try to implement and experiment whatever I have seen in my mom’s kitchen

This space, I am always sharing fresh, flavorful, (mostly) healthy recipes that I love to make and eat in my real, every day life. If I wouldn’t eat it in real life, I won’t put in on the blog.

My goal is to inspire you with food that is both healthy AND exciting, whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, your roommates, or your friends.

On a related note, I absolutely LOVE seeing the food that you’re making. It will make my day if you tag @homecooking_comfortfood in your Instagram photos and stories! Also convey me your experiences at Contact

Along with all the recipes, I also use the blog to share bits and pieces of my life outside the kitchen – from photos of my favorite trips.

I also share about my life on my personal instagram @love_whatyoulive (Vrushali Bhagat) I’d love if you follow me there too!
