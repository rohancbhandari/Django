Zucchini And Beetroot Brownies

May 18, 2020

Ingredients
  • 2 cups zucchini finely chopped or grated
  • 1 cup cooked beets pureed
  • 1 and 1/4 cup sugar/ brown sugar/ jaggery
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 cup neutral flavored oil like vegetable or canola or butter or ghee
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt

  2. Chop zucchini in a food processor or by hand with a cheese grater and place in a large mixing bowl
  3. Puree cooked beets and add them to the zucchini
  4. Sprinkle sugar over veggies and stir with a spoon
  5. Add remaining ingredients and stir slowly until mix well and it creates a thick batter
  6. Spray oil on a 9x13 baking dish or rub with a coating of any neutral flavored oil
