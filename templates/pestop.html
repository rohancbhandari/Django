PESTO CHICKPEA FUSILLI

June 10, 2020

Ingredients for Pesto Sauce
  • 3/4 cup pine nuts/ walnuts
  • 4 garlic cloves/li>
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan-Reggiano or Romano cheese freshly grated
  • 3 cups basil leaves packed
  • 1 cup spinach packed
  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice Â 
Directions
  1. Add all ingredients except for basil ,spinach and olive oil to food processor
  2. Blend with pulsing motion for a minute or so to breakdown and form a paste
  3. Next add the basil and with processor running slowly add the oil in a steady stream to blend thoroughly
  4. This makes 3 cups of fresh basil pesto which can be used over hot pasta or as a dipping sauce
Directions for Pasta
  1. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water until done. Drain.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a frying pan over medium low heat.
  3. Add garlic, pesto, onion, your choice of veggies , salt and pepper. Cook about five minutes, or until veggies are soft.
  4. Mix pesto mixture into pasta. Stir in grated cheese. Serve.
