APRICOT CAKE WITH ALMOND, COCONUT

May 14, 2020

Ingredients
  • Fresh apricots 6
  • Almond flour *(100g)and whole wheat flour (160g)
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2/3 cup coconut sugar/ sugar 130g
  • 3/4 cup soy milk 180ml, or other non-dairy milk( If you want you can use regular milk )
  • 3 tbsp non-dairy yogurt 45g, (or regular plain yogurt)
  • 1/2 cup oil 120ml, or vegan butter
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice or apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest optional
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 tbsp shredded coconut optional
Directions
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C). Lightly grease the sides of a 9-inch pan and line the bottom with parchment paper.
  2. Cut the apricots into halves and remove the core. If they’re not fully ripe, cut into quarters or thinner slices.
  3. In a mixing bowl whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and sugar.
  4. To a measuring jar add the non-dairy milk, yogurt, oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, and vanilla extract. Mix to combine.
  5. Pour the wet mixture into the flour mixture and stir using a spatula just until combined. Fold in shredded coconut as desired.
  6. Transfer the batter to the prepared pan. Place the sliced apricots on top, then sprinkle with coconut flakes, almonds and brown sugar.
  7. Bake for 50-60 minutes or until a skewer inserted in the center of the cake comes out almost clean with just a few crumbs attached .
  8. Let cool before serving. Enjoy!
