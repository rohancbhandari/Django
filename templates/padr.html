Pad Thai & Tomato Basil Soup

June 4, 2020

Ingredients for Pad Thai
  • Brown rice noodles
  • 1 cup tofu
  • 1/4 cup bean sprouts
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 tablespoons oil
  • 1/4 cup peanuts, chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh herbs like cilantro, green onions, and basil, chopped
Ingredients for Sauce
  • 3 tbsp vegan tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp white vinegar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili paste
Directions
  1. Spiralize the red pepper, carrots and green onions into noodle-like shapes
  2. Shake up the sauce ingredients in a jar.
  3. Heat 1/2 tablespoon of oil over medium high heat. Add the veggies â€“ stir fry with t for 2-3 minutes or until tender-crisp . Also saute tofu. Transfer to a dish and set aside.
  4. Add another 1/2 tablespoon of oil to the pan. Drain the noodles
  5. Add the noodles to the hot pan and stir fry for a minute, Add the sauce and stir fry for another minute or two, until the sauce is starting to thicken and stick to the noodles.
  6. Add in the vegetables, pan fired tofu, bean sprouts toss together, and remove from heat
  7. Stir in the peanuts and herbs and serve immediately
